I am a first-year Psychology major at UCR. I attended high school at Granada Hills Charter High School. At the moment, my plans for the future are unclear, as I have been rethinking my end career goals, but I am considering going to graduate school (hopefully in LA County) to obtain my master's. Right now, I am looking into pursuing a career as a creative director! Ultimately, I want to fulfill my dreams of working in LA County. In addition, I also want to hold a seasonal job Halloween season that pertains to storytelling, visual or otherwise, preferably at a theme park!

In the long term, I aspire to utilize the knowledge and skills I will learn in my psychology major, as well as my years in college more generally, to become a creative director. I understand that a creative director is a position that one usually has to work up to, but I am always prepared to put in the effort. Additionally, I plan to put together a portfolio of my creative and academic endeavors during my years in college to further my chances of success in my chosen field, alongside attending grad school to strengthen my skills. I found I enjoy envisioning ideas and sharing them with and for a larger community, as well as collaborating with others to push some of these projects to fruition.

Outside of my academic and career goals for my future, I consider myself to be a generally carefree person. I want to lead a life for myself that is ultimately stress-free (or at least live life with reduced stress on a day-to-day basis) and live how I want to. I am aware that not everything I may envision for myself may work out in the future, as nothing ever works out perfectly, by which I mean, how one initially envisions, as well as because goals can change and the future is uncertain. Therefore, since I am also a pretty flexible person (though I generally prefer to stick to a concrete plan), I plan to explore my passions and experience more in the moment and let that encapsulate the future I strive to build for myself.

Though I do not currently have any work experience, I have served my community through doing volunteer hours. I have done a variety of community service, ranging from assisting with marathons to helping out at Dodger games! Additionally, I have a decent amount of experience with design. I practice traditional design via the production of artwork on paper and do it as a means to expand my creative skillset. I also enjoy writing stories in my free time to better my writing skills and have past experience as a writer for the school's yearbook (middle school) and IB program newsletter (high school). Further, taking Computer Science 6 at UCR Winter quarter has taught me how to develop websites using CSS and HTML. I hope to put these skills to use in my job in the future, whether it is at a technology company or an entertainment company, working my way towards the title of creative director.
